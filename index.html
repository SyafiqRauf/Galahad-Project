Key Features

Galahad Finance's key features combine advanced technology, affordability,
security, and user-friendliness to create a powerful and inclusive financial platform.

0% Interest Rate

One of the standout features of Galahad Finance is its 0% interest rate on borrowed funds. This means that users can access loans without incurring any additional cost, making them highly attractive for borrowers. The 0% interest rate sets Galahad Finance apart from many other lending platforms that typically charge interest on borrowed funds

Secure

Galahad Finance places a strong emphasis on security . Galahad Finance will be audited multiple times by multiple top notch audit firm with reputable track records. Users can trust that their assetsand transactions are protected by the platform’s security measures

Yield Bearing

Galahad Finance introduces a unique opportunity for users to earn yield while borrowing against their Ethereum holdings. By utilizing the LSD protocol on Arbitrum, users can generate passive income from their deposited assets. The yield-earning mechanism enables users to optimize their borrowing strategy and enhance their overall profitability within the Galahad Finance ecosystem.

0% Loan Fee

In addition to the 0% interest rate, Galahad Finance charges 0% loan fees to borrowers. This means that users can access loans without incurring any upfront fees, further enhancing the cost-effectiveness and attractiveness of borrowing through Galahad Finance.

Minimum Collateral of 150%

To ensure the security and stability of its lending platform, Galahad Finance requires borrowers to provide collateral worth at least 150% of the borrowed amount. This collateralization ratio mitigates the risk of default and safeguards lenders' funds.By maintaining a minimum collateral requirement, Galahad Finance ensures a prudent lending environment for both borrowers and lenders.
